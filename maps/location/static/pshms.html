P.S.MATRIC.HIGHER.SECONDARY.SCHOOL

P.S. Matriculation Higher Secondary School is a co-educational institution in Mylapore, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, offering schooling from Junior Nursery to Standard XII. It was established in 1988 as part of the P.S. Educational Society. The school provides a comprehensive academic curriculum and organizes various co-curricular activities. History: The school was founded in 1905 in three rented premises in Mylapore. In 1928, it moved to its current location on Brodie's Road. The school became a higher secondary school in 1978. The school's name, P.S. Higher Secondary School, honors its founder, Pennathur Subramania Iyer. P.S. Senior Secondary School (CBSE) was established as a unit of the P.S. Educational Society in 1976.
